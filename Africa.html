PORT ELIZABETH

Port Elizabeth is an industrial city between Cape Town and Durban within Nelson Mandela Bay. The terminal features a museum displaying original art from the 1960s and literature from the original library.On board the QE2, visitors can explore 13 decks featuring numerous options for dining , lounge , live shows etc.
The heritage remains throughout the ship, with some of the early restaurants still in place – like the Queens Grill which has been refurbished into a modern bistro. Across 10 unique venues, passengers can enjoy the QE2’s hospitality in a multitude of styles. With even more attractions set to open, this nautical gem is bound to navigate guests through the culture and charm of the mid-1900s.
